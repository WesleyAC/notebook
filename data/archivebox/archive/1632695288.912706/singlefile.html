Recurse Center

Due to the spread of COVID-19 and its effects on the global economy and our business, we are operating online until further notice.

What we do. The Recurse Center is a self-directed, community-driven educational retreat for programmers based in New York City and currently operating online.

Our philosophy. We believe people learn best when they take control of their own education and are free to explore what theyre interested in. RC is heavily influenced by unschooling.

Never graduate. RC doesnt end after you leave. We have a diverse, active, and engaged alumni community of over 1,900 smart, enthusiastic, helpful programmers all over the world.

Build a career. Were here to support you at every stage of your career, whether youre looking for a new job or not, and whether you just finished RC or attended many years ago.

Hire great programmers. RC has an integrated recruiting agency. We help companies of all sizes hire our alumni. If you care about building productive, diverse teams, get in touch.

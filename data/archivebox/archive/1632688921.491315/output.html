Bring Your Vaccine Cards!

There are new rules from the CDC and the state of New York concerning vaccinated individuals. If you are visiting Wonderville and have proof of vaccination (either your card, a photo of your card, or the Excelsior Pass) please bring it along! We now require proof of vaccination to enter.

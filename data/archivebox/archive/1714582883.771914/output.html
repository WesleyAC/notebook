Clean & Simple

Designed for creative fixing, making and DIY projects, Sugru is super easy to use and control. This award-winning mouldable glue is mess-free and more versatile and creative than any other adhesive – with some pretty mind-blowing features and benefits.

Fix & Make

Out of the pack, Sugru is soft and malleable, just like play-dough. Roll the material in your fingers, press it where you need it, and mould it by hand into any shape. Whether it’s repairing a broken zipper or making protective rubber feet for chairs, you have 30 minutes to fix, bond, seal, mount, modify, create, decorate, improve, upcycle, even personalise – and more than enough time to shape, reposition and make perfect.

Mouldable & Flexible

Leave for 12–24 hours. Thanks to some clever science and advanced technologies, Sugru reacts at room temperature simply on exposure to air. It crosslinks into a strong, flexible, tactile silicone rubber. Sugru will flex with the materials it’s applied to, so you can fix things that need to bend. It works like nothing else on leathers, fabrics, or flexible plastics.

Waterproof & Temperature-resistant

Once set, Sugru is waterproof, long-lasting and happy in the heat and the cold, just like other silicone rubbers. You can use it in challenging environments like wet bathrooms, hot showers and sink areas – even outdoors with long-term exposure to the sun, sea and other weather conditions.

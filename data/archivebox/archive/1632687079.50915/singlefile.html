Online Application

  • Introduction
  • Employment Gold Card

INFORMATION

  • Foreign special professionals who plan to work in Taiwan can apply to National Immigration Agency for 4-in-1 employment gold card which includes work permit, resident visa, Alien Resident Certificate and re-entry permit.

  • The Employment Gold Card is valid for 1-3 years.

  • Those who are qualified can reapply before the expiration date of current gold card.

QUALIFICATION

  • Professional fields under qualification for employment gold card are:Science and technology, economy, education, culture, arts, sports, finance, law and architectural design.

  • Click for detailed qualification to each field (hyperlink)

  • This platform does not support China UnionPay card and American Express card. Only JCB, VISA, MasterCard credit cards are accepted for online payment. No refunds will be made after the payment is completed.

  • After the payment is completed, the case will be directed to the review and verification stage.

Entry certificate and receipt download

  • After the case is approved, the applicant will receive an email of approval notification sent automatically by the system. For overseas application, you may operate the "Download e-Receipt" and "Download e-Entry permit" functions in the "Download Area" to obtain the electronic files.

  • The e-Entry permit is only for overseas applicants to download, and must be printed in color and used for the first time upon arrival.

how does this work?

When you input a word, Metaphoria selects a common poetic symbol and pairs it with your word to create a novel seed metaphor, e.g. if you input friendship you might get the seed metaphor friendship is a stream.

This seed metaphor is expanded by looking for different ways these two words might be related. Each relation can be further expanded to find more words related to your concept or to the poetic symbol.

The goal is that the algorithmic companion provides you with unexpected but relevant ideas to help you create beautiful and original metaphors.

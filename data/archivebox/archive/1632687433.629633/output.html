Do you need further assistance? 

About Employment Gold Card
Please check out the Taiwan Employment Gold Card website to find out more about the program and application process. Check out the FAQ as well! 
Taiwan Employment Gold Card website：https://goldcard.nat.gov.tw/
If you have any further questions and suggestions, please e-mail to：
help@taiwangoldcard.tw

About Foreign Professionals
Please check out the Contact TAIWAN website to find out more about work permits, visas, ARCs, and other information on living in Taiwan.  
Contact TAIWAN website：https://www.contacttaiwan.tw
If you have any further questions and suggestions, please go to Contact TAIWAN/About Us/Contact Us and submit your question.

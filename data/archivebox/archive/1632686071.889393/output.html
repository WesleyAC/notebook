SimplyE: NYPL’s E-Reader App

  • Borrow E-Books and Audiobooks

    Developed by The New York Public Library, SimplyE is a free, open source e-reader app that allows us to bring together our entire collection of more than 300,000 e-books and audiobooks and make them accessible all in one place.

Get a Digital Library Card Through SimplyE

  • How to Apply: A Step-by-Step Guide

    New York state residents can apply for a New York Public Library card through SimplyE to access a wide array of digital resources, including e-books, audiobooks, and databases available from home—plus borrow physical materials through our temporary Grab & Go service. Please note that you must be within New York State when applying in order to fully access and complete the application.

    Learn more about our Grab & Go service.
